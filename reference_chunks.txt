[CSA_PLAYBOOKS]
CSA Singapore Incident Response Playbooks provide practical response workflows for malware, ransomware, phishing, and account compromise scenarios.

[NIST_IR]
NIST SP 800-61r2 defines incident response phases: Preparation, Detection & Analysis, Containment, Eradication, Recovery, and Post-Incident Activity.

[MITRE_ATTACK]
MITRE ATT&CK maps adversary tactics and techniques such as Credential Access, Initial Access, Lateral Movement, and Impact.

[SOC_SOP]
Enterprise SOC SOPs define alert triage, escalation, analyst handoff, SLA tracking, evidence preservation, and stakeholder notification.
